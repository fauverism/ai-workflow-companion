# 60-Second Demo Script (screen-share walkthrough)

Target: ~150 spoken words. One take, no jump cuts needed. [Bracketed] = on-screen action.

---

**0:00–0:08 — Cold open on the Today view**

[App open on Today: three reps, streak, heatmap visible]

"This is Workflow Companion. Three small reps a day for building an agentic engineering practice — because reading about workflows doesn't change how you work. Reps do."

**0:08–0:20 — Today**

[Hover the reps, check one off, point at heatmap]

"Each rep comes from your current phase — today: start a session with explicit acceptance criteria. Check it off, streak continues, and the heatmap fills in like a contribution graph."

**0:20–0:35 — Ladder**

[Switch to Ladder, scroll Crawl → Walk → Run]

"Progress isn't a feeling — it's this ladder. Crawl, Walk, Run. Every milestone is observable: three slash commands in git, fifteen CLAUDE.md rules from real corrections. Hit one, it's saved with the date."

**0:35–0:48 — Toolkit**

[Switch to Toolkit, open /techdebt, hit copy]

"The Toolkit is copy-paste ready. This is /techdebt — an end-of-session sweep for shortcuts. One click, one file, checked into your repo, your whole team gets it."

**0:48–0:60 — Prompt Lab + close**

[Switch to Prompt Lab, fill Correction→Rule, show output]

"And the Prompt Lab turns the core prompts into fill-in-the-blanks — here's correction→rule. No accounts, no backend, everything in localStorage. Link below — start at Crawl."

---

**Recording notes**

- Pre-fill the Prompt Lab example before recording so 0:48 is a reveal, not typing.
- Have at least a few weeks of heatmap data seeded (or your real usage) — an empty heatmap undercuts the habit story.
- If it runs long, cut the second sentence of the cold open first.
